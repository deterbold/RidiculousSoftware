# Privacy Policy

**ridiculous.software** &mdash; Effective date: May 2026 &middot; Last updated: May 2026

---

## The short version

Ridiculous Software does not collect, store, or track any personal data. No analytics, no accounts, no cookies, no surveillance. Your business is your own.

> **Note on AI-powered apps:** Some apps send user input — such as text prompts or images — to Anthropic's API to generate responses. That data is governed by [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy). Ridiculous Software does not receive, log, or store any of it.

---

## Data collection

Ridiculous Software apps do not collect personal information of any kind. This includes, but is not limited to: names, email addresses, device identifiers, location data, usage analytics, and crash reports sent to our servers.

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking pixels.

---

## Third-party AI services

Some Ridiculous Software apps integrate with generative AI models provided by Anthropic, PBC. When you use these features, the content you provide — such as typed prompts or uploaded images — is transmitted directly to Anthropic's API in order to generate a response.

Ridiculous Software does not intercept, log, or retain this content. However, Anthropic may process and handle it according to their own terms. We strongly recommend reviewing:

- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy)
- [Anthropic Consumer Terms of Service](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/consumer-terms)

Each app that uses AI features will indicate this clearly within the app itself.

---

## Data storage

Any data you create or enter within a Ridiculous Software app stays on your device. Nothing is transmitted to our servers, because we do not operate any. App preferences, saved content, and local state are stored exclusively on your own device and are subject to your device's own security model.

---

## Children

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. Since we collect no information at all, this policy applies equally to everyone.

---

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update the effective date above. As long as the summary at the top still reads "we collect nothing," not much has changed.
