To accelerate your daily routines and maximise your reach, we introduce Take Maker: AI-powered software designed to give you all the  opinions you may ever need.

Take Maker is the app for those who want to have an opinion but are too busy building and innovating. Using the power of agentic AI, Take Maker allows the user to quickly type, dictate, or take a picture of the topic they want to have a take in, adjusting the target audience and the tone to get the perfect result. Whether you are engaging with your business partners, your groupchat, or a potential partner, Take Maker will help you have an AI-powered opinion about anything.
